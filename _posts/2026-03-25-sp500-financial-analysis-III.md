### RSI (Relative Strength Index)

This is a **momentum oscillator** that measures the speed and change of price movements.

-   **Overbought (> 70):** When the blue line crosses the red dashed line, the stock has potentially risen too far, too fast, and a pullback or consolidation is likely.
    
-   **Oversold (< 30):** When it drops below the green line, the "selling pressure" may be exhausted, signaling a potential buying opportunity.
    
-   **Divergence:** Analysts look for cases where the price makes a "higher high" but the RSI makes a "lower high"—this is often a warning that the trend is losing steam.
