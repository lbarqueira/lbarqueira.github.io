Financial Information about S&P 500 (Standard and Poor's 500).

## 📊 1. What the lines mean

### 🔵 MACD Line

-   The difference between short-term and long-term momentum (typically 12-day EMA − 26-day EMA)
-   Reacts **quickly** to market changes

### 🟠 Signal Line

-   A smoothed version of the MACD (usually 9-day EMA of MACD)
-   Used to generate **buy/sell signals**

----------

## 🔁 2. Key Signals to Watch

### ✅ Bullish signal

-   When **MACD crosses ABOVE the signal line**
-   Suggests upward momentum is building

### ⚠️ Bearish signal

-   When **MACD crosses BELOW the signal line**
-   Suggests downward momentum is increasing

### ➕ Above zero

-   Market has **overall bullish momentum**

### ➖ Below zero

-   Market has **overall bearish momentum**

----------
