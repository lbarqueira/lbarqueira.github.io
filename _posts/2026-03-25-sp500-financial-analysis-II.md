### MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)

This tells you about **trend strength and direction changes**.

-   **Crossovers:** When the blue MACD line crosses _above_ the orange Signal Line, it’s a bullish signal. When it crosses _below_, it’s bearish.
    
-   **Zero Line:** When the MACD is above zero, the short-term momentum is stronger than the long-term momentum (bullish).
    
-   **The Gap:** The distance between the two lines shows the strength of the trend. A widening gap means the trend is accelerating.
